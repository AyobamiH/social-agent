# Quality Checks

## Scoring Rubric

Score every platform draft from 1 to 5 on:

- specificity
- human tone
- platform fit
- clarity
- practical consequence
- non-genericity

Reject or revise anything under:

- 4 on specificity
- 4 on human tone
- 4 on platform fit

## Universal Reject Reasons

Revise if the draft:

- sounds generic
- sounds transferable to any industry
- sounds like documentation instead of a post
- carries more than one core idea
- loses the deeper problem from the source
- loses the practical consequence
- uses banned phrases

## Automation Workflow

1. Ingest source
2. Extract source summary
3. Choose one platform angle
4. Draft natively for the platform
5. Strip banned phrasing
6. Score the draft
7. Revise if needed
8. Finalize
